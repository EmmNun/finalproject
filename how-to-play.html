How to Play Volleyball Like a Pro

Basic Skills

Serving

Start with a solid stance, toss the ball consistently, and strike with a firm wrist.

Passing

Use your forearms to create a platform, keep elbows straight, and angle your platform to target.

Setting

Form a triangle with your fingers, cushion the ball, and push it with your fingertips.

Attacking

Approach with proper footwork, jump vertically, and snap your wrist on contact.

Blocking

Read the setter, time your jump, and penetrate over the net with hands.

Digging

Stay low, react quickly, and use proper platform or overhead technique.
